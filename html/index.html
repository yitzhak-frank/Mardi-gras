Welcome To Mardi Gras New Orleans

A Few Words

The Mardi Gras parade that takes place every year in New Orleans is one of the largest in the world and is without a doubt the most beautiful, with an abundance of colors, costumes, flowers, and smiling people, the celebrations spread over several days and include dozens of performances and events.
New Orleans is currently in Phase II of our reopening with most hotels, restaurants and attractions open with extensive Covid-19 safety measures in place. While we expect to celebrate Mardi Gras in 2021 and are making plans as usual, chances are won’t have a definitive answer from the Mayor's office before early fall. When we know, you’ll be the first to know. We are sure that everything will work out and the parade will take place as every year, we have prepared a list of hotels, bars And museums for your convenience, so check the weather, dress accordingly and go out to explore our stunning city :)
